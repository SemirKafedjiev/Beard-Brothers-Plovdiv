ABOUT US

BEARD-BROTHERS-PLOVDIV is located in Plovdiv best kept secret, “Shepherd Market”, in a wonderfully charming small square hidden between Piccadilly and Curzon Street in the exclusive Borough of Mayfair. Mayfair itself is named after the infamous 15 days fair that took place on the site that is now known as Shepherd Market. It was developed in 1735-46 by Edward Shepherd. It comprises of a number of small side streets with a variety of boutique shops, restaurants and impressive pubs.
